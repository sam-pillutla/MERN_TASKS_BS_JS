MATERIAL CARDS

BLUE
DIAMOND

Blue diamonds are exceptionally rare and prized for their deep blue color, making them among the world's most valuable gemstones.

AMETHYST

Amethyst is a highly valued purple quartz gemstone, cherished for its captivating violet hue and spiritual significance. It is renowned for promoting clarity and inner harmony.

RED
RUBY

Red ruby, a precious gemstone, is celebrated for its vibrant crimson hue and is often associated with love and passion. It's considered one of the most coveted gemstones.

GOLD

Gold, a precious metal, has been treasured throughout history for its rarity and enduring value, making it a symbol of wealth and prosperity worldwide.
